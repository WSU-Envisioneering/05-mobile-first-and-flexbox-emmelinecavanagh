About the Movie

“Avatar: The Way of Water” is set more than a decade after the battle and when some of the humans are sent back to Earth in the first film, it explores the story of the Sully family and their battle to protect their home beneath Pandora’s oceans.

Main Characters

  • Jake Sully – The ex-Marine and Olo'eyktan of the Omaticaya clan. Father of Neytam, Lo'ak, Tuktirey, and the adoptive father of Kiri. Known as Toruk Makto
  • Neytiri – Jake’s wife and fierce protector of Pandora. Mother of Neytam, Lo'ak, Tuktirey, and the adoptive mother of Kiri.
  • Kiri – Adoptive daughter of Jake Sully and Neytiri, with a mysterious strong connection to Eywa. Born from Grace Augustine's dormant Avatar. She is the biological daughter of Grace Augustine's avatar.
  • Lo'ak – Second son of Jake Sully and Neytiri.
  • Neytam – The first and eldest child of Jake Sully and Neytiri.
  • Tuktirey – Yougest child of Jake Sully and Neytiri. She is independent and demanding.
